Getting Started

To get started with the CACD Platform, follow these steps:

  1. Create a new project by entering a project name and configuring the necessary settings.
  2. Use the "Fine Tuning" options to customize the behavior of your language model.
  3. Train your custom chatbot using the provided form.

By following these steps, you will be able to set up your chatbot project and begin tailoring it to your specific needs. The platform's intuitive interface makes it easy to configure settings and fine-tune your model.

Fine Tuning Options

The Fine Tuning section allows you to adjust advanced settings for your model:

  • Top-P: Controls the cumulative probability threshold for token sampling.
  • Top-K: Controls the number of highest probability tokens to keep for sampling.
  • Temperature: Controls the randomness of predictions by scaling the logits before applying softmax.

These options give you precise control over the behavior and output of your chatbot, allowing you to fine-tune its responses to meet your specific requirements. Experiment with different settings to find the optimal configuration for your use case.
